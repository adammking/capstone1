What is Depression?

Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest. According to the latest results of the National Survey on Drug Use and Health (2018) 17.7 million (7.2%) of American adults experienced a major depressive episode. There are many factors that influence the onset and severity of a depressive episode.
